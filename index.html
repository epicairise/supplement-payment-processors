Frequently Asked Questions

Which payment processors are safest for supplement subscriptions?

Processors like EasyPayDirect, Soar Payments, and Durango specialize in high-risk verticals and offer recurring billing support, chargeback protection, and fast approvals.

Can I accept crypto for my supplement store?

Yes. Platforms like PaymentCloud and NOWPayments support crypto, giving you global payment flexibility and lower processing fees.

How do I avoid getting banned by Stripe or PayPal?

Use vetted high-risk processors who understand your industry. These services manually review your business and support continuity models that Stripe often flags.
