Frequently Asked Questions

Which payment processors support recurring billing for supplements?

Top processors like EasyPayDirect, PayKings, and Soar Payments offer recurring billing tailored for supplement brands, with tools to minimize chargebacks and approvals delays.

Can I use crypto payment gateways for health product subscriptions?

Yes. Options like NOWPayments and PaymentCloud allow crypto payments for recurring supplement subscriptions, including support for compliance concerns.

What happens if Stripe flags my store?

High-risk processors like Durango and Corepay are equipped to onboard stores banned by Stripe or PayPal, offering manual underwriting and continuity model support.

Do these processors work with Shopify or WooCommerce?

Most support major platforms including Shopify, WooCommerce, and custom integrations — ideal for ecommerce supplement sellers needing stable recurring billing.

Is manual underwriting better for high-risk verticals?

Yes. Manual underwriting is preferred for supplements and nutraceuticals to ensure compliance, reduce holds, and prevent unexpected account shutdowns.

How fast can I get approved?

Approval times vary, but providers like Soar Payments and EasyPayDirect often complete underwriting and onboarding in as little as 24–48 hours.
